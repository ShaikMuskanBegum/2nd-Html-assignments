About

With over 24 years of experience, we have been successful in putting smiles on the faces of children and their parents. We have given the joy of motherhood at BirthRight and fulfilled the dream of parenthood for couples at BirthRight Fertility. We are a leading chain of multi-specialty pediatric, and OB/GYN hospitals in India, operating 19 hospitals and 3 clinics in 6 cities, with a total bed capacity of 1900 + beds. Our Pediatric services include newborn and pediatric intensive care, pediatric multi-specialty services and pediatric quaternary care (including multi-organ transplants). Our OB/GYN services include normal and complex obstetric care, multi-disciplinary fetal care, perinatal genetic and fertility care. Along with parents, doctors play a big part in caring for children. Together with a team of specialized doctors and medical personnel, we aspire to continue treating your little ones like our own, giving them dedicated care and attention.
